To the One Who Owns My Heart

My love, I know I’ve hurt you more times than I ever should have, and for that, I’m deeply sorry. You are everything to me—my sunshine, my laughter, the peace to my chaos. Every moment without you feels like a chapter missing from the story of my life.

I’ve made mistakes, but please believe me when I say I never meant to make you feel unloved or unimportant. You are the most beautiful soul I’ve ever known, and I cherish every little thing about you—from your smile to your spark, your moods, and even your silence. I miss it all.

This website is my way of trying to express what my words couldn’t say in person. I want to remind you of how deeply I love you, and how much I’m willing to fight for us. I’m not perfect, but my love for you is unwavering and sincere.

Please scroll through, see the moments we’ve captured, and feel the love I’ve poured into every page. I hope this brings even the smallest smile to your beautiful face.
